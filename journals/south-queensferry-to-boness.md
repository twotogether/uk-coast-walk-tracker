### Places Along the Way

**South Queensferry**
   - Public toilets on the High Street
   - Excellent bakeries, cafes, and restaurants
   - Good public transport links to Edinburgh

**Blackness**
   - No public toilets (2025)
   - Excellent pub called The Lobster Pot where we had a pint
   - No public transport on weekends

**Bo'ness**
   - Public toilets on [Register Street](https://maps.app.goo.gl/nLf3B6bicVLHXhMd8) and in the Tesco Superstore
   - There are some bakeries and cafes
   - Public transport available from Edinburgh, Stirling, Dunblane, and other places in West Lothian
